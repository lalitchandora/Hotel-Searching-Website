Get the celebrity treatment with world-class service at JW Marriott Mumbai Sahar Airport

Located 400 metres from Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport Mumbai and offering an outdoor swimming pool, fitness centre and a spa wellness centre, JW Marriott Mumbai Sahar is located in Andheri East.
Each room here will provide you with air conditioning and a minibar. Featuring a bath or shower, private bathroom also comes with a hairdryer and bathrobes. Interconnecting/ family rooms are available at a request.
At JW Marriott Mumbai Sahar you will find a 24-hour front desk and 24-hour in-room dining. Other facilities offered at the property include a executive lounge.
The Juhu Beach and the ISKCON Temple are 8 km, the Goregaon Film City is 16.4 km and the Sanjay Gandhi National Park is 20 km away. The Marol Bus Station is 2.4 km, Marol metro Station is just 1.2 km and the Andheri Railway Station is 4.4 km. The Mumbai Domestic Airport Terminal is at a distance of 3.6 km from the hotel.
Sample a mélange of cuisines, including authentic Italian, Japanese or classic Indian at any one of the hotel's restaurants.
Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 9.0 for a two-person trip.
